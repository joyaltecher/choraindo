Why Choraindo?

We are a dedicated matching network designed specifically for youngsters and college students to make emergency blood donation fast, personal, and impactful.

Rapid Matches

Register as a donor or submit a request. Our real-time dashboard links you with seekers in your immediate college campus or city neighborhood instantly.

Campus Network

Mobilizing college clubs, hostels, and youth associations to create a reliable and active community pool of healthy, ready donors.

Direct Connect

No middlemen or blood banks. Seekers contact donors directly to arrange donations at verified hospitals in the fastest time possible.

Eligibility Check

To ensure the safety of both donors and recipients, verify these conditions:

  • You are between 18 and 65 years old.
  • You weigh at least 50 kg (110 lbs).
  • You haven't donated blood in the last 90 days.
  • You are not under any active antibiotics.

Your Privacy Matters

We do not sell your personal data. Your contact details will only be accessible by verified seekers looking for blood matches on our dashboard. You can toggle your availability or remove your profile at any time.
